HOW TO PLAY

SOLSTICE is a light-routing puzzle. Use mirrors to guide sunbeams and moonlight to their receptors.

CLICK an empty cell to place a / mirror. Click again to rotate to \. Click a third time to remove.

RIGHT-CLICK any mirror to remove it directly.

TOGGLE between SUN and MOON mode. Each activates different emitters.

Route both beams to their targets using the same mirror setup to complete the level.

Complete all levels to unlock the Turing Test terminal.
